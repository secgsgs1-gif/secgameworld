Dig Miner

Mine blocks, collect ores, and sell loot for points. (Manual mine sell reward x3)

Points: 0

Depth: 0

Pickaxe: Lv.1

Facing: DOWN

Tickets: 0/3

Next Ticket: -

Move with WASD / Arrow keys, mine forward with Space.

How To Play

  • Press Start Mining to use 1 ticket and begin the session.
  • Move with W/A/S/D or Arrow Keys.
  • Mine the block in front of you with Space.
  • Sell Inventory converts mined items to points.
  • Rare ores: MYTHRIL, AETHER (harder than DIAMOND).
  • New Mine creates a new random map during an active session.
